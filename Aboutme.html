About Me

One of my favourite past-times is getting lost in the worlds of fantasy and sci-fi novels, movies and games. I have gained a deep appreciation for the immersive capability of computer graphics. My Passion

On the Gallery page, you can view personal projects that I have created using the program Blender. (some may be labelled placeholders)

Blender is a 3D creative software that allows modelling, rigging, animation, simulation, rendering, compositing, motion tracking, and much more. Blender is the go-to for beginners in 3D graphics. It serves as an all-purpose, artistic and professional design medium. It is also completely free, open source, and has a more flexible and beginner-friendly interface than comparable software like Maya.

My website is as a platform for learners and general users of Blender. Specifically, it is for those who want to discovering interesting elements and techniques that they can apply in their own projects, and perhaps also learn skills while doing so.

The Guidebook contains a compilation of hand-picked beginner-friendly tutorials where the objective is to inspire creativity. I believe that the best way to learn is as consequence of doing something you enjoy, rather than for its own sake. You could bore yourself following a 10 hour, ad-filled tutorial course to create a regular glazed donut, or you could spend a few hours creating this awesome wireframe donut.

Each post includes my own description of the element, and an embedded link to the tutorial.
